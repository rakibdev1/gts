LANDLORD CERTIFICATES

By law, landlords need to ensure their property is safe and provide electrical certificates for their electrical system and all appliances. To make sure you are compliant with current legislation and that your property is safe for your tenants, two tests will need to be carried out. An Electrical Installation Condition Report (EICR) will need to be carried out every five years, and Portable Appliance Testing (PAT) will need to be carried out every year. Failure to obtain EICR and PAT certificates can invalidate your landlord’s insurance and even lead to prosecution

EICR Certificate

The EICR Certificate covers any part of the electrical system that’s served from wiring connected to the electric meter. One of our fully qualified electricians will test every aspect of your electrical system and make recommendations on anything unsatisfactory.
