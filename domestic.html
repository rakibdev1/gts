domestic electricians

‘Domestic’ covers a huge area from checking or rewiring your home to the electrical installations for a complete new housing development.

your home

Even a keen DIY enthusiast usually draws the line at wiring. Dabbling with electrical fittings can be fatal if you don’t know what you’re doing. When you need an expert who can provide you with the necessary fully-certified skills to put your mind at rest, you’ll find our team are experienced and easy-to-deal with. From small jobs such as fitting new sockets or rewiring lights to complete electrical refits for extensions and conversions, nothing is too much trouble.

Services for landlords

If you’re a landlord and want to ensure that your properties are inspected regularly and appliances are tested, our electricians can supply the necessary landlords certificated and carry out portable appliance tests (PAT). If you want an electrician that you – and your tenants – can trust, you’ll find our team are reliable, trustworthy experts and tidy up after themselves too.
