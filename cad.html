Software We use

For lighting design and particularly lux level calculations, we use DIALux and AGi32. For power and load calculations, we use PowerCAD, AutoCAD or Revit to create electrical drawings. We also utilise the latest technology to communicate and share files using web-based online collaboration systems, such as BIM Collaborate Pro, as well as sophisticated, web-based markup tools.
